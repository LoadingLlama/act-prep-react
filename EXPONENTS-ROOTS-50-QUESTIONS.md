## HARD QUESTIONS (35-50)

### Question 35 (Hard)

**Simplify: (2x^3y^2)^3 ÷ (4x^2y)^2**

A) (x^5y^4)/2
B) 2x^5y^4
C) x^5y^4
D) (x^5y^4)/4

**Solution (Answer: A)**

**Expand both expressions.**
```
(2x^3y^2)^3 = 8x^9y^6
(4x^2y)^2 = 16x^4y^2
```

**Divide.**
```
8x^9y^6 ÷ 16x^4y^2 = (8/16) × x^(9-4) × y^(6-2)
= (1/2) × x^5 × y^4
= x^5y^4/2
```
