Content Guidelines

  • Natural Science: Scientific processes, discoveries, natural phenomena. Technical but accessible.

Content Replication Guide

Data Representation (5 questions per passage)
  • Include 2-3 figures (graphs, tables, or diagrams)
  • Focus on a single experiment or dataset
  • Questions test basic data reading and trend identification
  • Common domains: Chemistry, Biology, Physics, Earth Science
Research Summaries (6-7 questions per passage)
  • Describe 2-3 related experiments
  • Include methods, results, and data presentation
  • Questions test experimental design understanding and comparison
  • Build progressive complexity across experiments
Conflicting Viewpoints (11-13 questions)
  • Present 2-3 different perspectives on a scientific topic
  • Each viewpoint should be 150-200 words
  • Questions compare viewpoints and test comprehension
  • Use current scientific debates (evolution, climate, etc.)
