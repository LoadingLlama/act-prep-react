Mastery Check Quiz: Areas, Volumes & Triangles

Question 1 (Easy)

A rectangle has a length of 9 inches and a width of 6 inches. What is its area in square inches?

A. 15

B. 30

C. 45

D. 54

E. 63

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Area of rectangle = length × width

A = 9 × 6 = 54 square inches

Question 2 (Easy)

A triangle has a base of 8 cm and a height of 5 cm. What is its area in square centimeters?

A. 13

B. 20

C. 26

D. 32

E. 40

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

Area of triangle = ½ × base × height

A = ½ × 8 × 5 = ½ × 40 = 20 cm²

Question 3 (Easy)

A cube has an edge length of 4 meters. What is its volume in cubic meters?

A. 12

B. 16

C. 32

D. 48

E. 64

Show Solution

Correct Answer: E

Volume of cube = s³

V = 4³ = 4 × 4 × 4 = 64 m³

Question 4 (Medium)

A right triangle has legs of length 5 and 12. What is the length of the hypotenuse?

A. 7

B. 10

C. 13

D. 15

E. 17

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Recognize the 5-12-13 Pythagorean triple!

Or use a² + b² = c²: 5² + 12² = 25 + 144 = 169, so c = 13

Question 5 (Medium)

A cylinder has a radius of 3 inches and a height of 8 inches. What is its volume in cubic inches?

A. 24π

B. 48π

C. 64π

D. 72π

E. 96π

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Volume of cylinder = πr²h

V = π(3)²(8) = π(9)(8) = 72π in³

Question 6 (Medium)

A. 6

B. 6√2

C. 6√3

D. 9

E. 12

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

In a 45-45-90 triangle, the ratio is x : x : x√2

Question 9 (Hard)

A right triangle has a hypotenuse of 17 and one leg of length 8. What is the length of the other leg?

A. 9

B. 12

C. 13

D. 15

E. 25

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Recognize the 8-15-17 Pythagorean triple!

Or use a² + b² = c²: 8² + b² = 17²

64 + b² = 289

b² = 225, so b = 15

Question 10 (Hard)

A cone has a radius of 6 cm and a height of 8 cm. What is its volume in cubic centimeters?

A. 48π

B. 72π

C. 96π

D. 144π

E. 288π

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Volume of cone = (1/3)πr²h

V = (1/3)π(6)²(8) = (1/3)π(36)(8)

V = (1/3)(288π) = 96π cm³
