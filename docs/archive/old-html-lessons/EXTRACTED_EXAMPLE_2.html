Example 3: Variables in Answer Choices

If x + y = 10 and x − y = 4, what is x2 − y2?

A. 6
B. 14
C. 40
D. 100
E. 116

Solution:

  • From the two equations, solve to get x = 7, y = 3
  • Calculate target: x2 − y2 = 72 − 32 = 49 − 9 = 40 ✓
  • Notice: This also equals (x + y)(x − y) = 10 × 4 = 40

Answer: C
