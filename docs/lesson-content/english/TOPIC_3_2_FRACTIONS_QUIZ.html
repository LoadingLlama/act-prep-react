Mastery Check Quiz: Fractions

Question 1 (Easy)

What is 3/8 + 1/8?

A. 4/16

B. 4/8

C. 1/2

D. 3/16

E. 2/8

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Same denominator, so add numerators: 3/8 + 1/8 = 4/8

Simplify: 4/8 = 1/2

Question 2 (Easy)

Multiply: 2/5 × 3/4

A. 5/9

B. 6/20

C. 3/10

D. 5/20

E. 6/9

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Multiply numerators and denominators: (2×3)/(5×4) = 6/20

Simplify by dividing both by 2: 6/20 = 3/10

Question 3 (Easy)

Divide: 1/2 ÷ 1/4

A. 1/8

B. 1/6

C. 1

D. 2

E. 4

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Keep, change, flip: 1/2 ÷ 1/4 = 1/2 × 4/1

= 4/2 = 2

This makes sense: "How many quarters fit in a half?" = 2

Question 4 (Medium)

What is 2/3 + 3/4?

A. 5/7

B. 5/12

C. 1 1/12

D. 1 5/12

E. 17/12

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Find LCD of 3 and 4: LCD = 12

2/3 = 8/12 (multiply by 4/4)

3/4 = 9/12 (multiply by 3/3)

8/12 + 9/12 = 17/12

Convert to mixed number: 17/12 = 1 5/12

Question 5 (Medium)

Simplify: 5/6 - 1/3

A. 1/6

B. 1/3

C. 1/2

D. 2/3

E. 4/3

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

LCD of 6 and 3 is 6

5/6 stays as 5/6

1/3 = 2/6 (multiply by 2/2)

5/6 - 2/6 = 3/6 = 1/2

Question 6 (Medium)

What is 3/8 × 4/9?

A. 1/6

B. 7/17

C. 12/72

D. 1/3

E. 12/17

Show Solution

Correct Answer: A

Cancel before multiplying: 4 and 8 have common factor 4

3/8 × 4/9 = 3/(2) × 1/9 (after canceling)

Also cancel 3 and 9: common factor 3

= 1/2 × 1/3 = 1/6

Question 7 (Medium)

Convert 2 3/5 to an improper fraction.

A. 10/5

B. 11/5

C. 13/5

D. 8/5

E. 23/5

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Formula: (whole × denominator) + numerator, all over denominator

= (2 × 5) + 3 / 5

= (10 + 3) / 5

= 13/5

Question 8 (Hard)

What is 1 1/2 × 2 2/3?

A. 3 1/6

B. 4

C. 4 1/3

D. 2 2/6

E. 3 2/6

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

Convert to improper fractions:

1 1/2 = 3/2

2 2/3 = 8/3

Multiply: 3/2 × 8/3 = 24/6

Notice 3's cancel: (3×8)/(2×3) = 8/2 = 4

Simplify: 24/6 = 4

Question 9 (Hard)

Simplify: 3/4 ÷ 1/2 + 2/3

A. 1 5/6

B. 2

C. 2 1/6

D. 11/6

E. 13/6

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Step 1: Division first (keep, change, flip)

3/4 ÷ 1/2 = 3/4 × 2/1 = 6/4 = 3/2

Step 2: Now add 2/3

3/2 + 2/3, LCD = 6

3/2 = 9/6

2/3 = 4/6

9/6 + 4/6 = 13/6

Step 3: Convert to mixed number

13 ÷ 6 = 2 remainder 1

13/6 = 2 1/6

Question 10 (Hard)

Which of the following is equivalent to (3/4 - 1/6) × 2/5?

A. 7/60

B. 7/30

C. 14/60

D. 7/15

E. 5/12

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

Step 1: Simplify inside parentheses (3/4 - 1/6)

LCD of 4 and 6 is 12

3/4 = 9/12

1/6 = 2/12

9/12 - 2/12 = 7/12

Step 2: Multiply by 2/5

7/12 × 2/5 = 14/60

Step 3: Simplify (divide by 2)

14/60 = 7/30
