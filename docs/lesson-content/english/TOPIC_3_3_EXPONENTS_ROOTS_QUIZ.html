Mastery Check Quiz: Exponents and Roots

Question 1 (Easy)

Simplify: 3² × 3³

A. 3⁵

B. 3⁶

C. 9⁵

D. 9⁶

E. 243

Show Solution

Correct Answer: A

Product rule: Same base, add exponents

3² × 3³ = 3^(2+3) = 3⁵

Note: 3⁵ = 243, but the simplified form is 3⁵

Question 2 (Easy)

What is 5⁰?

A. 0

B. 1

C. 5

D. Undefined

E. 10

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

Zero exponent rule: Any nonzero number to the power of 0 equals 1

5⁰ = 1

Question 5 (Medium)

Simplify: (2³)²

A. 2⁵

B. 2⁶

C. 4⁵

D. 4⁶

E. 6²

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

Power rule: Power to a power, multiply exponents

(2³)² = 2^(3×2) = 2⁶

Note: 2⁶ = 64

Question 6 (Medium)

What is 2^(-4)?

A. -16

B. -8

C. 1/8

D. 1/16

E. 1/4

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Negative exponent rule: a^(-n) = 1/a^n

2^(-4) = 1/2⁴ = 1/16

Common mistake: Negative exponent doesn't make the answer negative!

Question 8 (Hard)

Simplify: (3x²y)³

A. 3x⁶y³

B. 9x⁶y³

C. 27x⁵y³

D. 27x⁶y³

E. 27x⁸y⁴

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Distribute the exponent to each factor:

(3x²y)³ = 3³ × (x²)³ × y³

= 27 × x⁶ × y³

= 27x⁶y³

Remember: For x², multiply exponents: (x²)³ = x^(2×3) = x⁶

Question 9 (Hard)

Evaluate: 16^(3/4)

A. 2

B. 4

C. 8

D. 12

E. 64

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Fractional exponent: a^(m/n) means take nth root, then raise to m power

16^(3/4) = (⁴√16)³

⁴√16 = 2 (since 2⁴ = 16)

2³ = 8

Question 10 (Hard)

Rationalize and simplify: 12/√6

A. 2√6

B. 6√2

C. 2√3

D. 4√6

E. 12√6

Show Solution

Correct Answer: A

Step 1: Rationalize by multiplying by √6/√6

12/√6 × √6/√6 = (12√6)/6

Step 2: Simplify

(12√6)/6 = 2√6
