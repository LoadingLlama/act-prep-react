Question 4 (Medium)

What is (-4) × (-3) + (-2) × 5?

A. -22

B. -2

C. 2

D. 10

E. 22

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

(-4) × (-3) = 12 (same signs = positive)

(-2) × 5 = -10 (different signs = negative)

12 + (-10) = 2

Question 5 (Medium)

Simplify: 3(x + 4) - 2(x - 1)

A. x + 10

B. x + 14

C. 5x + 10

D. 5x + 14

E. x + 2

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

3(x + 4) = 3x + 12

-2(x - 1) = -2x + 2

3x + 12 - 2x + 2 = x + 14

Question 6 (Medium)

Simplify: 18 - 6 ÷ 2 + 3²

A. 15

B. 18

C. 21

D. 24

E. 30

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Exponents: 3² = 9 → 18 - 6 ÷ 2 + 9

Division: 6 ÷ 2 = 3 → 18 - 3 + 9

Left to right: 18 - 3 + 9 = 15 + 9 = 24
