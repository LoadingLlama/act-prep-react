Mastery Check Quiz: Arcs and Sectors

Question 1 (Easy)

A circle has a central angle of 40°. What is the measure of the arc intercepted by this angle?

A. 20°

B. 40°

C. 80°

D. 320°

E. 360°

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

The measure of an arc equals the measure of its central angle.

Central angle = 40°, therefore arc measure = 40°

Question 2 (Easy)

A circle has a radius of 6 cm. What is the length of an arc with a central angle of 60°?

A. π cm

B. 2π cm

C. 3π cm

D. 6π cm

E. 12π cm

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

Arc Length = (θ/360°) × 2πr

Arc Length = (60°/360°) × 2π(6)

Arc Length = (1/6) × 12π = 2π cm

Question 3 (Easy)

A sector of a circle with radius 10 inches has a central angle of 36°. What is the area of the sector?

A. π in²

B. 5π in²

C. 10π in²

D. 20π in²

E. 100π in²

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Sector Area = (θ/360°) × πr²

Sector Area = (36°/360°) × π(10)²

Sector Area = (1/10) × 100π = 10π in²

Question 4 (Medium)

An arc with a central angle of 120° has a length of 8π feet. What is the radius of the circle?

A. 6 feet

B. 8 feet

C. 10 feet

D. 12 feet

E. 16 feet

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

Arc Length = (θ/360°) × 2πr

8π = (120°/360°) × 2πr

8π = (1/3) × 2πr

8π = (2πr)/3

24π = 2πr

r = 12 feet

Question 5 (Medium)

Convert 150° to radians.

A. π/6 radians

B. π/3 radians

C. 2π/3 radians

D. 5π/6 radians

E. 5π/3 radians

Show Solution

Correct Answer: D

To convert degrees to radians, multiply by π/180°

150° × (π/180°) = 150π/180 = 5π/6 radians

Question 6 (Medium)

A circular pizza with diameter 14 inches is cut into 7 equal slices. What is the area of one slice?

A. 7π in²

B. 14π in²

C. 49π/7 in²

D. 196π/7 in²

E. 49π in²

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Diameter = 14 inches, so radius = 7 inches

Total area = πr² = π(7)² = 49π in²

One slice = Total area ÷ 7 = 49π/7 = 7π in²

Alternative: Central angle = 360°/7, Sector Area = (360°/7)/360° × 49π = 49π/7 = 7π in²

Question 7 (Medium)

A clock has a minute hand that is 12 inches long. How far does the tip of the minute hand travel in 15 minutes?

A. 3π inches

B. 6π inches

C. 9π inches

D. 12π inches

E. 24π inches

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

In 15 minutes, the minute hand travels 15/60 = 1/4 of the circle

Central angle = 360° × (1/4) = 90°

Arc Length = (90°/360°) × 2π(12) = (1/4) × 24π = 6π inches

Question 8 (Hard)

A sector of a circle with radius 9 cm has an area of 27π cm². What is the measure of its central angle?

A. 60°

B. 90°

C. 120°

D. 135°

E. 150°

Show Solution

Correct Answer: C

Sector Area = (θ/360°) × πr²

27π = (θ/360°) × π(9)²

27π = (θ/360°) × 81π

27 = 81θ/360°

27 × 360° = 81θ

9720° = 81θ

θ = 120°

Question 9 (Hard)

A circle has a radius of 15 meters. Using radians, find the arc length subtended by a central angle of 2π/5 radians.

A. 6 meters

B. 6π meters

C. 30 meters

D. 30π meters

E. 75 meters

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

When using radians, Arc Length = rθ

Arc Length = 15 × (2π/5) = 30π/5 = 6π meters

Question 10 (Hard)

A sector has a central angle of 150° and an arc length of 10π cm. What is the area of this sector?

A. 30π cm²

B. 60π cm²

C. 75π cm²

D. 120π cm²

E. 144π cm²

Show Solution

Correct Answer: B

Step 1: Find the radius using arc length

Arc Length = (θ/360°) × 2πr

10π = (150°/360°) × 2πr

10π = (5/12) × 2πr

10π = (5πr)/6

60π = 5πr

r = 12 cm

Step 2: Find sector area

Sector Area = (150°/360°) × π(12)²

Sector Area = (5/12) × 144π = 60π cm²
