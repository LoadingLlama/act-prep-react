2. Hyperbolas

Hyperbolas rarely appear on the ACT. In recent years, the ACT has been adding more questions with advanced topics, so we certainly expect hyperbolas to appear more frequently on future ACTs. If you see any hyperbolas questions on the ACT, you need to be familiar with the equations for vertical and horizontal

hyperbolas and how to graph a hyperbola on the coordinate plane.
