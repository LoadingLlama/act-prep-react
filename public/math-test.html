Algebra

Slope: m = (y₂ - y₁) / (x₂ - x₁)
Distance: d = √[(x₂ - x₁)² + (y₂ - y₁)²]
Midpoint: ((x₁ + x₂)/2, (y₁ + y₂)/2)

Trigonometry
